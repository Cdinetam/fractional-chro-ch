FAQ on the Fractional CHRO Model

Is a Fractional CHRO only suitable for large companies?

No. SMEs, scale-ups, and growth-stage companies in particular benefit from this flexible C-level model.

How does this differ from interim management?

Interim roles are often transitional. Fractional engagements are longer-term, more strategic, and more deeply integrated into executive leadership.

Does a Fractional CHRO relieve the CEO?

Yes. Strategic people leadership is represented at C-level, while the CEO is relieved in key HR-related decision areas.
